Take a gentle pause in your busy daily life.

'Yoonseul Fishing' is a 2.5D low-poly mindfulness fishing app named after the beautiful Korean word 'Yoonseul'—which represents the sparkling ripples of sun or moonlight dancing on the water surface.

There are no complex requirements, no competition, and no in-app purchases. Simply throw your bobber into the water, listen to the real-time synthesized atmospheric sounds (pentatonic chimes, rain, wind, crickets), and peacefully wait for a bite.

Features:
- Dynamic Yoonseul Visuals: Watch the sky and water colors blend smoothly across day, sunset, and starry night phases.
- Procedural Audio Engine: Background sounds are generated entirely in real-time, matching weather patterns with soothing major pentatonic chimes.
- 12 Unique Fish Species: Discover various creatures from the golden crucian to the legendary moonlight catfish and the mythical starlight whale.
- Upgrades & Collection: Earn coins, buy specialized baits, level up, and complete your fish encyclopedia.
